About jess.foo

This is a personal site designed for use in ESL classrooms. As someone with qualifications in education, web development and machine learning, my ultimate goal is to venn-diagram those three realms and build a bespoke resource for my ESL students to enjoy.

Initially, this site will provide a collection of external resources that I reckon students will find useful. As time goes, I plan to add additional site functionality, along with access to applications I've built myself. It might not happen overnight, but it will happen! üòÖ
